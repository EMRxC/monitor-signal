# Monitor-Signal-Clinical-Pilot-Test
Centralized monitoring analytics for clinical trials — KRI computation, site-level anomaly detection, and LLM-drafted risk/status narratives with documented human oversight. Built on CDISC SDTM pilot data.
Building toward a working demonstration of risk-based quality management (RBQM) analytics, built by a Senior CRA with 12 years of site monitoring experience — bridging clinical operations judgment and the data tooling that's reshaping traditional monitoring.
