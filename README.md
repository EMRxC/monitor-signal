# monitor-signal
Centralized monitoring analytics for clinical trials — KRI computation, site-level anomaly detection, and LLM-drafted risk narratives with documented human oversight. Uses CDISC SDTM pilot data.
A working demonstration of risk-based quality management (RBQM) analytics from a Senior CRA with 12 years of site monitoring experience — bridging clinical operations judgment and the data tooling reshaping traditional monitoring.
